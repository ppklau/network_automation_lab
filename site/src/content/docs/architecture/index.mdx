ACME Investments operates across four regions connected by iBGP. The London DC1 fabric is fully instantiated in the lab. New York, Singapore, and Frankfurt run as FRR stub border routers — they participate in BGP and accept Ansible pushes, but do not have a full DC behind them.

Click any region node to drill down.

## BGP ASN allocation

| Region | Site | ASN |
|--------|------|-----|
| EMEA | London DC1 | 65001 |
| EMEA | London DC2 (SoT only) | 65002 |
| Americas | New York DC1 | 65010 |
| Americas | New York DC2 (SoT only) | 65011 |
| APAC | Singapore | 65020 |
| APAC | Hong Kong (SoT only) | 65021 |
| EU Regulatory | Frankfurt | 65030 |
| Branches | UK (per-office) | 65100–65111 |
| Branches | US (per-office) | 65120–65127 |

Frankfurt peers only with London — never directly to New York or APAC. This topological constraint enforces the EU data residency requirement: EU-origin traffic cannot transit non-EU routing paths.
